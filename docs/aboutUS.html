Welcome to SoundBase,

SoundBase is a platform for talented independent artists to share their music to the world. Developed by young students of MindX, SoundBase aims to transform the habit of music sharing by making it convenient and easy to access. With just a click, your personal song is shared on our platform, ready to be heard among millions of users. We continuously update SoundBase leaderboard. 10 artists with highest contributions will receive an honor gift from the developers. Be ready because the prize is going to be BIG.
